MY TV SHOWS

COMIC ADAPTATION

Smallville (2001)

A teenage Clark Kent struggles to balance the typical angst of high school with his developing alien abilities.

Directed by: Greg Beeman

Starring: Tom Welling as Clark Kent

The Flash (2014)

After being struck by lightning during a particle accelerator explosion, forensic scientist Barry Allen wakes up with the gift of superhuman speed.

Directed by: Eric Wallace

Starring: Grant Gustin as Barry Allen

Anime

Kengan Ashura (2019)

A mysterious fighter named Ohma Tokita enters an underground world where corporate disputes are settled by brutal gladiator brawls.

Directed by: Seiji Kishi

Voice Actor: Tatsuhisa Suzuki as Tokita Ohma

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners (2022)

A street kid named David Martinez loses everything to a sudden tragedy in the sprawl of Night City.

Directed by: Hiroyuki Imaishi

Voice Actor: Kenichiro Ohashi as David Martinez

Thriller

The Walking Dead (2010)

Sheriff’s Deputy Rick Grimes leads a group of survivors in a desperate search for safety.

Created by: Frank Darabont

Starring: Andrew Lincoln as Rick Grimes

Yellowjackets (2021)

A high school girls' soccer team survives a plane crash deep in the remote northern wilderness.

Directed by: Karyn Kusama

Starring: Melanie Lynskey as Shauna Shipman

Cartoons

Ben 10 (2005)

Ten-year-old Ben Tennyson discovers a mysterious alien watch called the Omnitrix.

Directed by: Kelly Crews and Donna Smith

Voice Actress: Tara Strong as Ben Tennyson

Teen Titans (2003)

Robin, Starfire, Cyborg, Raven, and Beast Boy—unite to protect Jump City from a new generation of villains.

Directed by: Scooter Tidwell

Voice Actor: Scott Menville as Robin

Fantasy

House of the Dragon (2022)

Directed by: Miguel Sapochnik

Starring: Emma D'Arcy as Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen

The Witcher (2019)

Directed by: Alik Sakharov

Starring: Henry Cavill as Geralt of Rivia
